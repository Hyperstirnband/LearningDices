The story of the App

I learned Portuguese in many ways. Language learning apps that offered lessons, with a teacher, watching subtitled movies, reading books for children and traveling are just a few of them. I always felt like talking was the most beneficial to me, if you have someone who corrects you and pays attention to talking correctly himself it is the fastest way for me to learn grammar.
I recently came across story cubes as a creativity tool in my work as a product manager, on my next vacation I used them with a friend who wanted to learn English, since her vocabulary was not broad enough talking in day to day activities was not working very well. The story cubes gave some sort of small context that we could talk about. It went something like this:
1. Roll the dices
2. Ask for meaning of the dices symbols
3. Start putting together a story and ask for more vocabulary if needed or whatever you are missing to make the story you got in mind
4. The native speaker then corrects and tells the story in his words

I learned many phrases and enhanced my feeling for times and learned a few very specific words. Back home I felt like I want to continue learning this way but it can be hard to meet up with tandem partners. Luckily we have the internet and now this app offering a new way to learn a language and chat with native speakers!
